About US

GSE (General Software Engineering) is established in 1995 in California, Sand Diego S.A. Since, the company faced many ups and downs, we grow our experience. This is why nowadays our company is one of the best choices for getting own software. In 2001 GSE started to produce microchips for Nasa. After 2 years the project was closed because of a low budget and not enough prime technology. But time has changed. After 2011 we started producing microchips for DARPA (Defense Advanced Research Projects Agency), in the security field. Besides the other fields, GSE can produce more than 5 software. In 2015 the company was awarded by Oracle for great partnership in data science.
